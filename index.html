About Us

Africa Women Land Rights and Agro Ecology Initiatives (AWLRAEI) is a non-profit organization committed to advancing women's land rights, agroecological farming, environmental conservation, climate resilience, and sustainable livelihoods throughout Africa.

Vision

A just and sustainable Africa where women have secure land rights, practice resilient agroecology and lead thriving communities.

Mission

To empower women and communities through secure land rights, agroecological farming, climate resilience, gender equality and leadership development.

Our Goal

Building resilient communities where women become leaders in sustainable agriculture, environmental conservation and policy decision-making.

Core Values

Gender Equality

Promoting equal rights and opportunities.

Justice & Equity

Fair access to land and livelihoods.

Sustainability

Protecting natural resources.

Empowerment

Building leadership and economic capacity.

Integrity

Transparency and accountability.

Collaboration

Working together with communities and partners.

Innovation

Encouraging local solutions.

Human Rights

Respecting dignity and inclusion.

Strategic Objectives

  1. Advocate for equitable land rights for women.
  2. Promote agroecological farming.
  3. Strengthen women's leadership.
  4. Enhance climate resilience.
  5. Improve livelihoods through entrepreneurship.
  6. Build partnerships for research and advocacy.
  7. Raise awareness on women's rights and environmental justice.

Our Programmes

Land Rights Advocacy

Creating awareness on women's land rights, providing legal support, promoting equitable land governance and influencing policy reforms.

Agroecology

Training farmers on sustainable agriculture, regenerative farming, organic production, composting and biodiversity conservation.

Climate Action

Tree planting, agroforestry, watershed management, environmental conservation and climate resilience initiatives.

Women's Empowerment

Entrepreneurship, financial literacy, leadership development and market access for women.

Community Development

Capacity building, youth empowerment, research, advocacy and knowledge sharing.

Partnerships

Collaborating with governments, NGOs, development partners and communities to create sustainable impact.

Who We Serve

Women Farmers

Supporting women with secure land rights, sustainable agriculture and improved livelihoods.

Rural Women & Widows

Empowering women facing barriers to land ownership and productive resources.

Youth

Providing agribusiness, climate-smart agriculture and leadership opportunities.

Community Groups

Supporting women's groups, cooperatives and farmer organizations.

Marginalized Communities

Working with vulnerable households, indigenous people and persons living with disabilities.

Environmental Champions

Partnering with schools and community organizations promoting conservation.
